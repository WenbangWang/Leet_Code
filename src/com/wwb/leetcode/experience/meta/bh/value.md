⚡ **How to use this sheet:**

* Pick **8–10 strong STAR stories** from your career.
* Map each one across multiple categories (e.g., the same “cross-team initiative” story could fit *Move Fast*, *Collaboration*, or *Focus on Impact* depending on how you tell it).
* Prepare **reflections** for each story:

  * *What would you do differently?*
  * *What did you learn?*
  * *How did it make you a better engineer/leader?*
